Education

I am currently getting a PhD at the University of Pittsburgh, where I work with Professor Robert Gaunt. Here, I study epidural spinal cord stimulation for lower urinary tract control.

Prior to joining this lab, I worked as a Research Technician at Northwestern University with Professors Lee Miller and Matthew Tresch. I studied functional electrical stimulation to restore locomotion after spinal cord injury, and (motion tracking, record brain signals from motor cortex, etc)

I graduated from Goshen College Summa Cum Laude in 2015 with a major in Physics and minors in Mathematics, Informatics, and Visual Art.
