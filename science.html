Neurostimulation and rehab

My current PhD work focuses on restoring lower urinary tract function. About 1 in 5 people will experience impaired bladder function, but despite this widespread impact it is often overlooked. Poor bladder function can come from many neurological conditions, including spinal cord injury, Parkinson's Disease, and more. Below, I highlight several specific projects I have worked on in this lab and previously. For a complete set of publications, please see my Google Scholar page or check out my CV here. I am happy to send a copy of any papers on request.
