My current PhD work focuses on restoring lower urinary tract function. About 1 in 5 people will experience impaired bladder function, but despite this widespread impact it is often overlooked. Poor bladder function can come from many neurological conditions, including spinal cord injury, Parkinson's Disease, and more. Below, I highlight several specific projects I have worked on in this lab and previously. For a complete set of publications, *scholar page and cv link* I am happy to send a copy of any papers on request.

List of projects:

Impact of behavior on stimulaton-evoked lower urinary tract control

Computational modelling of sacral spinal cord stimulation

Predicting stimulation-evoked bladder function based on neural activity

Selective recruitment of lower urinary tract nerves with epidural spinal cord stimulation

DRG stuff
